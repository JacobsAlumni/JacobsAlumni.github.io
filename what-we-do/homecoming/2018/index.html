Homecoming 2018

Mark your calendar. Book your flight. Cancel that boring conference. Look for a babysitter. Take the Monday off. Start packing your suitcase: Save the date for the Homecoming 2018!

This year’s Homecoming will take place on September 21 – 23. In terms of events during the weekend you can expect a similar range to last year:

  • Friday: Open lectures, Career Symposium, Bar Chillout
  • Saturday: Big BBQ, General Assembly with updates from the Association and the University, Jacobs Games (Olympix), a Gala Dinner, Cheerleading Finale, a big theme party.
  • Sunday: Brunch and Farewell
